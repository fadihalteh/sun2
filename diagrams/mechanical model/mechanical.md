# 3RRS Mechanical Model | 3RRS 机械结构模型

[English](#english) | [中文](#中文)

---

## English

SolidWorks mechanical structure models for the Solar Stewart Tracker.

### File Formats

- `.SLDPRT` - SolidWorks part files
- `.SLDASM` - SolidWorks assembly files

### Main Components

#### Motion Platform System
- **The motion platform above.SLDPRT** - Upper motion platform
- **The static platform below.SLDPRT** - Lower static platform

#### Servo System
- **3230 Servo Motor.SLDPRT** - 3230 servo motor
- **Dual-axis servo.SLDASM** - Dual-axis servo assembly
- **Double-axis narrow U bracket.SLDPRT** - Dual-axis narrow U bracket
- **Double-axis wide U bracket.SLDPRT** - Dual-axis wide U bracket

#### Linkage Mechanism
- **L1 arm.SLDPRT** - L1 linkage arm
- **L2 arm.SLDPRT** - L2 linkage arm
- **ball head.SLDPRT** - Ball joint head
- **ball head neck.SLDPRT** - Ball joint neck

#### Steering Components
- **Real-axis steering wheel.SLDPRT** - Real-axis steering wheel
- **Imaginary axis steering wheel.SLDPRT** - Imaginary-axis steering wheel

#### Other Components
- **Raspberry Pi box sketch version.SLDPRT** - Raspberry Pi enclosure (sketch version)
- **sc-16-yp.SLDPRT** - SC-16-YP component
- **I M6.SLDPRT** - M6 fastener
- **di.SLDASM** - Main assembly

## 中文

太阳能 Stewart 追踪器的 SolidWorks 机械结构模型。

### 文件格式

- `.SLDPRT` - SolidWorks 零件文件
- `.SLDASM` - SolidWorks 装配体文件

### 主要组件

#### 运动平台系统
- **The motion platform above.SLDPRT** - 上层运动平台
- **The static platform below.SLDPRT** - 下层静态平台

#### 伺服系统
- **3230 Servo Motor.SLDPRT** - 3230 伺服电机
- **Dual-axis servo.SLDASM** - 双轴伺服装配体
- **Double-axis narrow U bracket.SLDPRT** - 双轴窄 U 型支架
- **Double-axis wide U bracket.SLDPRT** - 双轴宽 U 型支架

#### 连杆机构
- **L1 arm.SLDPRT** - L1 连杆臂
- **L2 arm.SLDPRT** - L2 连杆臂
- **ball head.SLDPRT** - 球头
- **ball head neck.SLDPRT** - 球头颈部

#### 转向组件
- **Real-axis steering wheel.SLDPRT** - 实轴转向轮
- **Imaginary axis steering wheel.SLDPRT** - 虚轴转向轮

#### 其他组件
- **Raspberry Pi box sketch version.SLDPRT** - 树莓派外壳草图版本
- **sc-16-yp.SLDPRT** - SC-16-YP 组件
